Salt and AirDAO Forge Strategic Partnership for Marketing Collaboration and DeFi Innovation

Salt is excited to announce a groundbreaking partnership with AirDAO, an emerging leader in decentralized finance (DeFi) and governance. This strategic collaboration aims to revolutionize the decentralized trading landscape, bringing together the unique strengths of both Salt and AirDAO to create greater opportunities for growth and user engagement.Together, Salt and AirDAO will launch joint marketing initiatives, allowing both platforms to reach new audiences, expand their ecosystems, and provide more value to their communities. As a result, users will benefit from an enhanced DeFi experience, access to exclusive events, rewards, and much more.

AirDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that leverages blockchain technology to create a community-centered environment for decentralized finance and governance. With a strong focus on transparency, scalability, and security, AirDAO enables users to actively participate in decision-making processes that shape the future of decentralized applications (dApps) and projects.

Moving Mina Forward — An Invitation

Mina’s succinct blockchain architecture means Mina stays accessible, decentralized, and sustainable over time — and opens up exciting opportunities to build a secure, democratic, user-centered future that connects crypto and the real world. On the architecture side, we’re exploring how to fulfill Mina’s potential as an open beacon of truth that’s accessible from any location and any device — from browsers to phones to smart homes, and for any user no matter where they are around the world.

Meanwhile, the Foundation will focus on Mina’s technical architecture, growing the ecosystem and partnerships, and on serving the community. The marketing, community, legal teams, and I will now channel our passion through the Foundation. I will be becoming the Mina Foundation’s CEO to lead our efforts under this new umbrella. We believe not just in the power of our technology, but in the power of our community. Our intention is to develop systems and structures for increasing self-organization — empowering the community to grow and scale, and putting the people using the protocol at the center of how decisions are made and resources are allocated.
